My 3 Favorite Films

1: The Lord of The Rings Trilogy

One of the best book to film adaptations that has ever been made.
Superb acting, great sets and locations, and the storyline is a classic.
One of the best retellings of the hero's journey that has been done.

2: House of Leaves

This book is so creative in it's usage of the layout of the text to convey the emotion
of the book. It's depiction of a broken home resonates deeply with me. The meloncholic
wandering of the narrator, the desperate obsession with unreality of the "hero."
It sits right in my heart.

3: Blame!

The manga is stellar. The architecture is impossibly large, foreboding, and entirely hostile.
The main character is on a impossible task, simply wandering from place to place as tragedy
follows in his wake. Though it's hard to say if that isnt simply the norm for the world.
The fact that so little is ever resolved, far more questions are asked than are ever answered.
It is absolutely one of my favorites.
